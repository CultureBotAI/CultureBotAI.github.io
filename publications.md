## üìÑ Preprints

### kg-microbe: A Comprehensive Knowledge Graph for Microbial Cultivation
**Joachimiak, M.P. et al.** (2025)  
*bioRxiv preprint*  
[https://doi.org/10.1101/2025.02.24.639989](https://www.biorxiv.org/content/10.1101/2025.02.24.639989v1)

**Abstract:** We present kg-microbe, a comprehensive knowledge graph that integrates diverse microbial data sources to enable AI-driven insights for cultivation and growth prediction. The knowledge graph combines taxonomic, genomic, metabolic, and environmental data from major biological databases, providing a unified resource for computational microbiology applications.

**Key Contributions:**
- Integration of 10+ major biological databases
- Semantic data organization using biological ontologies
- Machine learning models for growth condition prediction
- Open-source tools and APIs for community access
